What to say when something is true?

There are those moments where you feel that something has touched your heart and you want others to know about it. Mention what caused yourself to feel such a touching moment and why it means so much to you. If people do not believe in the strong feeling you have, continue to convince them as it is insulting to you to not be believed. If someone does not understand you, don’t take offense and say that they will get you someday
